Discover Monopoli

Monopoli represents, on the Adriatic, one of the most active and densely populated ports of the Apulia region high walls.

A labyrinth of alleys, alleys and squares in which a historical-artistic heritage is concentrated.

Beautiful sea view rooms, along the wall, will make your walks moments of pleasure and relaxation.

Many coves, rocky or sandy, make Monopoli an enchanting city overlooking the sea and with a great history and life to discover. Visit it while staying in Cala Verdegiglio

Discover Puglia

Cala Verdegiglio Home, boasts a unique strategic position, just 7km you can reach Polignano a Mare, the famous town of Domenico Modugno, a unique village overlooking the sea. At 13km you can visit the Castellana Caves experiencing a unique moment of exploration. Just 15km away you can reach Fasano with its “Fortified Masserie” and the Zoo Safari, 19 Km away from Alberobello, a UNESCO world heritage site for its trulli.

Then Locorotondo, Ostuni "the white city", Cisternino and Martina Franca make Monopoli the right place to visit all the Apulian beauties.

Recommended itinerary

The recommended Itinerary for Visiting Monopoli starts from Largo Plebiscito continuing with the walk along Via Garibaldi, the ancient Via dei Mercanti, until reaching Piazza Garibaldi, a place steeped in history, which preserves remains from different eras. We recommend a visit to the Castello di Carlo V, part of the Spanish fortification system and located on one of the oldest areas of the old city of Monopoli.

Walking along the Lungomare you will admire the imposing ramparts and the panoramic view of the Porta Vecchia with the sixteenth-century fortified walls up to the Basilica of Maria SS. della Madia with its sumptuous interiors, built on a pre-existing Romanesque church, to which the legend of the Landing of the Icon of the Madonna della Madia from 1117 is linked.

Finally, you can admire the remains of the old Norman Castle and arrive in Piazza Vittorio Emanuele II, the beating heart of the new city of Monopoli.

How to reach Cala Verdegiglio Home

By car

From Bari SS 16, from Brindisi SS 379 Monopoli-Conversano exit until you reach the Municipal Stadium and take via Fiume. Public parking under the house and in the area (white stripes).

By plane

L’aeroporto internazionale di Bari-Karol Wojtyla is 59 km away (44 minutes by car), also connected by train or bus (Terravision, Pugliaairbus) which will take you directly to Bari Central Station in about 30 minutes. Alternatively we can organize a comfortabletransfer for you with a private driver for a fee.

L’aeroporto del Salento di Brindisi-Casale, is 73 km away (50 minutes by car), connected by shuttle bus to Brindisi Central Station.
